Common Cookies

Common Cookies are a type of cookies that could be received in the Cookie Gacha. They are typically the most common and the weakest cookies.

List of Common Cookies

This contains the list of all Common Cookies in Cookie Run: Kingdom.

GingerBrave Muscle Cookie Strawberry Cookie Wizard Cookie

Tips

Since common cookies are generally the weakest type in the game, they much aren't used in PvP gameplay.
However, having a good team in the start is neccesary. Here is a team recommendation from us.

Muscle Cookie is a good charge cookie, since he has good health and deals good damage with his skill.
Strawberry Cookie is a good defense cookie with one of the highest health cookies in the common cookie category.
Wizard Cookie is the good since he deals AoE damage
Beet Cookie is the only ranged cookie in the category, which makes her attack range very long.
And you could either use Ninja Cookie for extra damage, or Angel Cookie for healing, depends on the situation.

But after playing for some time, you'll get better cookies, which you can replace with the cookies mentioned.
