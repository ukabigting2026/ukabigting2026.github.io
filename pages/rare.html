Rare Cookies

Rare Cookie are a type of cookies that can be received in the Cookie Gacha. They're the second most common cookie type.

Tips

Since rare cookies are the second most common cookies in the game, they're also the second most weakest cookies, except for some.
Here is a team recommendation from us.

Since you'll get Custard Cookie III early in the game, he's one of the recommendations here, since he could heal other cookies and gives two shields at the two weakest cookies.
Knight Cookie is a good defense cookie since he could tank most of the damage.
Blackberry Cookie is one of the strongest cookie in this category.
Pancake Cookie can give additional attack speed to the team after using his skill.
And Cherry Cookie's skill deals high damage, therefore, she's on here.

But, like I said before, these cookies will be soon outclassed by higher tier cookies.
