Tips

Since there are multiple cookies here with multiple different skills, it could be difficult to choose to put into your team. So, to make things easier, I will choose the best of the best from the list.

Clotted Cream Cookie has a skill where it not only deal AoE damage, but jails one enemy cookie, making that cookie immobile and vulnerable.
Cream Unicorn Cookie's skill allows you to heal your team while disabling the opposing team for 3 seconds, allowing your team to deal extra damage.
Wildberry Cookie has devastating damage when his skill is activated.
Financier Cookiehas a skill where it heals their team and deal damage to enemies.
And Oyster Cookie is a great support since she can summon 4 "guards" that deal damage and tank damage
