Tips

Since you can only place up to 3 legendary or ancient cookies, it is really hard for a rookie player to choose which to place.
Luckily, there was a tier list showing which cookies are best and which are, garbage. So, here are the 3 best Legendary and Ancient Cookies.

Dark Cacao Cookie is the best charge cookie in the game, since he's tanky and deals high damage with his skill, as well making the enemy's cookies lose health permanently for the battle.
Sea Fairy Cookie is the best bomber cookie in the game, with one of the highest possible damage, which could reach over 200,000 damage. Pair this with her skill, which makes 5 water bursts, and expect the enemy health to fall down.
And Black Pearl is the best since she's got one of the best debuff in the game, the Terror of the Abyss. Pair this with her skill, and the enemy team will receive multiple debuffs.
